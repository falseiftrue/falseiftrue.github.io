PhD student @ UMSI

I am a third-year PhD student at the University of Michigan School of Information, where I am advised by Oliver Haimson and Eric Gilbert.

At the moment, I am interested in understanding the role of surveillance technologies in the enforcement of anti-trans laws and the oppression of trans people. You can email me about my work at flameiro at UM's domain.
