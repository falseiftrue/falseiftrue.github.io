TIDEs: Transphobia Identification in Digital Environments

TIDEs is a dataset of trans-related social media comments labeled for transphobic content. It was created by me and four RAs at the University of Michigan. Feel free to use it for your own academic or non-academic projects. You can read our full paper here.
